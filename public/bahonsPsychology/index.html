Overview of BA. (Hons) Psychology

The B.A. (Honors) Psychology program at ITM University, Raipur is research led and future oriented program that offers a vibrant environment and distinctive curriculum for undergraduate education in Psychology without parallel in Chhattisgarh and central India. The program is focused on developing new age generation of thought leaders in the field of Psychology & Behavioral Science. The program is delivered by the prominent academicians who are trained from some of the reputed academic institutions and research think tanks in India. The B.A. (Honors) Psychology curriculum will assist students in acquiring interdisciplinary knowledge to address practical issues in the area of their choice. Students are trained to think critically, analyze, and implement the scientific method.

Undergraduate students can major in psychology, cognitive science, neuroscience, counseling, forensic psychology, with additional opportunities to minor in human resource management etc. The curriculum covers the fundamentals of biological, developmental, cognitive, and abnormal psychology, along with specialized subjects like Health, Social, Clinical, Counseling, Forensic, Military, Sports and other emerging fields in psychology. The Learning Outcomes Based Curriculum Framework (LOCF) technique has been used to enhance students' learning experience as they involve themselves in their preferred course.
